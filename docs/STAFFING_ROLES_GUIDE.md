# MradiPro Staffing & Roles Guide

## Overview
This document outlines the key professional roles needed to operate and grow the MradiPro construction materials platform effectively.

---

## 🏢 CORE OPERATIONS TEAM

### 1. **Operations Manager** (1 person)
**Salary Range:** KES 80,000 - 150,000/month

**Responsibilities:**
- Overall platform operations oversight
- Coordinate between all departments
- Handle escalated issues from customers
- Monitor platform performance metrics
- Manage supplier and delivery provider relationships
- Ensure SLAs are met

**Skills Required:**
- Business management experience
- Strong communication skills
- Problem-solving abilities
- Knowledge of construction industry (preferred)

---

### 2. **Customer Support Representative** (2-3 people)
**Salary Range:** KES 30,000 - 50,000/month each

**Responsibilities:**
- Respond to customer inquiries (builders, suppliers, delivery providers)
- Handle the chatbot escalations ("Talk to Human" requests)
- Process complaints and resolve disputes
- Assist with order issues and tracking
- Onboard new users via phone/email
- Update FAQs based on common questions

**Skills Required:**
- Excellent communication (English & Swahili)
- Customer service experience
- Basic computer literacy
- Patience and empathy

**Shift Coverage:**
- Morning shift: 7 AM - 3 PM
- Afternoon shift: 3 PM - 11 PM
- Consider weekend coverage

---

### 3. **Supplier Relations Officer** (1 person)
**Salary Range:** KES 50,000 - 80,000/month

**Responsibilities:**
- Verify and onboard new suppliers
- Conduct supplier quality audits
- Negotiate pricing and terms
- Handle supplier complaints and issues
- Maintain supplier database accuracy
- Visit supplier locations for verification
- Ensure suppliers meet platform standards

**Skills Required:**
- Sales/account management experience
- Knowledge of construction materials
- Negotiation skills
- Willingness to travel within Kenya

---

### 4. **Delivery Coordinator** (1-2 people)
**Salary Range:** KES 40,000 - 60,000/month

**Responsibilities:**
- Monitor all active deliveries in real-time
- Coordinate between suppliers and delivery providers
- Handle delivery disputes and delays
- Assign deliveries to providers
- Track GPS and ensure accurate ETAs
- Process delivery confirmations
- Handle QR code scanning issues

**Skills Required:**
- Logistics/dispatch experience
- Ability to multitask
- Good under pressure
- Basic tech proficiency

---

## 💻 TECHNICAL TEAM

### 5. **Technical Support / IT Administrator** (1 person)
**Salary Range:** KES 60,000 - 100,000/month

**Responsibilities:**
- Monitor platform uptime and performance
- Handle technical issues and bugs
- Manage database backups
- Update platform content
- Coordinate with developers for fixes
- Manage user access and security
- Generate technical reports

**Skills Required:**
- Basic web development knowledge
- Database management (SQL)
- Problem-solving skills
- Experience with Supabase/cloud platforms (preferred)

---

### 6. **Full-Stack Developer** (Part-time/Contract)
**Salary Range:** KES 100,000 - 200,000/month (or hourly contract)

**Responsibilities:**
- Fix bugs and technical issues
- Implement new features
- Optimize platform performance
- Security updates and patches
- Database maintenance
- API integrations

**Skills Required:**
- React/TypeScript experience
- Supabase/PostgreSQL
- Node.js
- Mobile responsiveness
- Git version control

**Note:** Can be outsourced or hired on contract basis initially.

---

## 📊 BUSINESS & FINANCE

### 7. **Finance & Accounts Officer** (1 person)
**Salary Range:** KES 50,000 - 80,000/month

**Responsibilities:**
- Process payments to suppliers and delivery providers
- Handle invoicing and billing
- Track platform revenue and commissions
- Prepare financial reports
- Manage M-Pesa/payment integrations
- Handle refunds and disputes
- Tax compliance (KRA)

**Skills Required:**
- Accounting background (CPA preferred)
- Experience with M-Pesa business
- Excel/financial software proficiency
- Attention to detail

---

### 8. **Marketing & Growth Officer** (1 person)
**Salary Range:** KES 50,000 - 80,000/month

**Responsibilities:**
- Acquire new builders and suppliers
- Manage social media accounts
- Create marketing content
- Run digital advertising campaigns
- Attend construction industry events
- Build partnerships with construction companies
- Analyze user acquisition metrics

**Skills Required:**
- Digital marketing experience
- Social media management
- Content creation
- Understanding of B2B marketing
- Data analysis skills

---

## 🛡️ QUALITY & COMPLIANCE

### 9. **Quality Assurance Officer** (1 person - can be part-time)
**Salary Range:** KES 40,000 - 60,000/month

**Responsibilities:**
- Review monitoring service requests
- Create and send quotations to builders
- Verify material quality complaints
- Conduct platform quality checks
- Process monitoring service activations
- Handle dispute resolution
- Ensure regulatory compliance

**Skills Required:**
- Attention to detail
- Construction industry knowledge
- Report writing
- Conflict resolution

## 🎯 KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS (KPIs) BY ROLE

### Customer Support
- Response time < 5 minutes (chat)
- Response time < 2 hours (email)
- Customer satisfaction > 4.5/5
- Resolution rate > 90%

### Delivery Coordinator
- On-time delivery rate > 95%
- Delivery disputes < 5%
- Average dispatch time < 30 minutes

### Supplier Relations
- Supplier verification < 48 hours
- Supplier satisfaction > 4/5
- Active supplier retention > 85%

### Marketing
- Monthly new builder signups
- Cost per acquisition < KES 500
- Social media engagement rate > 3%

## 📍 OFFICE REQUIREMENTS

### Minimum Setup:
- Small office space (can start with co-working)
- Reliable internet connection
- Computers/laptops for each staff
- Phone lines for customer support
- Backup power (generator/UPS)

### Location Recommendations:
- Nairobi CBD or Westlands (accessible)
- Near construction material hubs (Gikomba, Industrial Area)

---

## 🔄 HIRING PRIORITY ORDER

1. **Operations Manager** - Leadership first
2. **Customer Support (2)** - Handle user queries
3. **Delivery Coordinator** - Manage logistics
4. **Finance Officer** - Handle money flow
5. **IT Support** - Keep platform running
6. **Supplier Relations** - Grow supplier base
7. **Marketing Officer** - Acquire users
8. **Additional Support** - Scale as needed

---

## 💡 COST-SAVING TIPS

1. **Start with multi-role employees** - Operations Manager can handle supplier relations initially
2. **Use remote work** - Reduce office costs
3. **Contract developers** - Don't hire full-time initially
4. **Automate with chatbot** - Reduce support load
5. **Partner with BPO** - Outsource after-hours support
6. **Interns/Fresh graduates** - Lower-cost support staff with training

---

## 📞 CONTACT STRUCTURE

### For Builders:
- Primary: Customer Support
- Escalation: Operations Manager

### For Suppliers:
- Primary: Supplier Relations Officer
- Escalation: Operations Manager

### For Delivery Providers:
- Primary: Delivery Coordinator
- Escalation: Operations Manager

### Technical Issues:
- Primary: IT Support
- Escalation: Developer (Contract)
