# MradiPro User Guide

## Welcome to MradiPro! 🏗️

MradiPro is Kenya's premier construction marketplace connecting builders with verified suppliers across all 47 counties.

---

## Getting Started

### 1. Create an Account

1. Visit [mradipro.com](https://mradipro.com)
2. Click **"Get Started"**
3. Choose your account type:
   - **Private Client** - For individual home builders
   - **Professional Builder** - For construction companies
   - **Supplier** - For material suppliers
   - **Delivery Provider** - For delivery services
4. Fill in your details and verify your email

### 2. Complete Your Profile

After signing up, complete your profile to unlock all features:
- Add your business details
- Upload verification documents
- Set your service areas (counties)

---

## For Builders

### Browsing Materials

1. Go to **Suppliers** page
2. Use filters to narrow down:
   - Category (Cement, Steel, Roofing, etc.)
   - County
   - Price range
3. Click on a material to see details

### Private Clients - Direct Purchase

1. Browse materials and click **"Add to Cart"**
2. Review your cart
3. Proceed to checkout
4. Enter delivery address
5. Select payment method
6. Confirm order

### Professional Builders - Request Quote

1. Browse materials and click **"Request Quote"**
2. Add items to your Quote Cart
3. Submit quote request to supplier
4. Wait for supplier to respond with pricing
5. Review quotes in your dashboard
6. Accept or reject quotes
7. Accepted quotes generate purchase orders

### Tracking Orders

1. Go to your **Dashboard**
2. Click on **Orders** tab
3. View order status and tracking information
4. Scan QR codes to verify deliveries

---

## For Suppliers

### Setting Up Your Store

1. Complete your supplier registration
2. Wait for admin approval
3. Once approved, access your **Supplier Dashboard**

### Adding Products

1. Go to **Products** tab
2. Click **"Add Product"**
3. Fill in:
   - Product name
   - Category
   - Description
   - Price and unit
   - Upload images
4. Submit for approval

### Managing Orders

1. Go to **Orders** tab
2. View incoming orders
3. Process and update order status
4. Generate delivery notes

### Responding to Quote Requests

1. Go to **Quotes** tab
2. View pending quote requests
3. Enter your pricing
4. Add any notes or terms
5. Submit quote

---

## For Delivery Providers

### Registration

1. Apply as a Delivery Provider
2. Submit required documents:
   - ID/Passport
   - Driving license
   - Vehicle registration
   - Insurance certificate
3. Wait for verification

### Accepting Deliveries

1. View available delivery requests
2. Accept requests in your service area
3. Pick up materials from supplier
4. Deliver to builder's site
5. Scan QR codes to confirm delivery
6. Collect proof of delivery

### GPS Tracking

- Your location is tracked during active deliveries
- Builders can see real-time delivery progress
- Ensure GPS is enabled on your device

---

## QR Code System

### For Suppliers (Dispatch)

1. When order is ready, go to **Dispatch Scanner**
2. Scan each item's QR code
3. Confirm items match the order
4. Generate dispatch note

### For Builders (Receiving)

1. When delivery arrives, go to **Receiving Scanner**
2. Scan each item's QR code
3. Verify quantities match
4. Report any discrepancies
5. Sign for delivery

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I verify my account?
Upload required documents in your profile. Admin will review and approve within 24-48 hours.

### Can I cancel an order?
Yes, you can cancel orders that haven't been dispatched. Go to Orders → Select Order → Cancel.

### How do I report a problem with delivery?
Use the Delivery Tracker to report issues. You can also contact support via live chat.

### Are prices negotiable?
Professional builders can request quotes for custom pricing. Private clients see fixed prices.

### How do I become a verified supplier?
Complete supplier registration with all required documents. Verification takes 2-5 business days.
