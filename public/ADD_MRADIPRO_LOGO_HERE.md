## 📋 Logo Specifications

- **File Name:** `mradipro-logo.png` (exactly this name)
- **Size:** 512x512 pixels minimum (1024x1024 recommended)
- **Format:** PNG with transparent or white background
- **Design:** Circular logo with:
  - "M" letter mark with blue swoosh
  - "MRADIPRO" text
  - "JENGA NA MRADIPRO" tagline
  - Construction-themed background

## 🎨 Color Scheme

- **Navy Blue:** #0F2C59 (Primary brand color)
- **Light Blue:** #4A9FD8 (Accent/secondary color)
- **Background:** White or transparent
