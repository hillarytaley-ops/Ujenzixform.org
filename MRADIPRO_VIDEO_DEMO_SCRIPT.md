## 🎨 **PRODUCTION NOTES**

### **Visual Style Guidelines**
- **Color Scheme:** Professional blues, greens, and Kenyan flag colors
- **Animation Style:** Clean, modern, smooth transitions
- **Typography:** Bold, professional fonts (Montserrat, Inter, Poppins)
- **Pace:** Dynamic but not rushed - allow features to breathe
- **Quality:** Minimum 1080p, ideally 4K for professional presentation

### **Music Recommendations**
- **Opening:** Energetic, problem-focused (minor key)
- **Solution:** Uplifting, confident (major key transition)
- **Features:** Steady, professional background
- **Closing:** Inspirational, crescendo to powerful finish

### **Voiceover Guidelines**
- **Gender:** Neutral/Male professional voice
- **Accent:** Kenyan English or neutral English
- **Tone:** 
  - Problem section: Concerned, empathetic
  - Solution section: Confident, exciting
  - Features section: Professional, informative
  - Closing: Inspiring, powerful

### **B-Roll Footage Needed**
1. **Construction Sites:**
   - Aerial drone footage of Kenyan construction sites
   - Workers in action (safety gear)
   - Materials being delivered
   - Construction progress time-lapse

2. **Platform Screens:**
   - Dashboard interactions (screen recordings)
   - Mobile app usage
   - Feature demonstrations
   - QR code scanning

3. **People:**
   - Professional builders reviewing plans
   - Suppliers managing inventory
   - Happy customers/testimonials
   - Team collaboration

4. **Kenya Locations:**
   - Nairobi skyline
   - Mombasa coast
   - Kisumu lakefront
   - Eldoret town
   - Rural construction sites
   - County diversity showcase

### **Screen Recording Checklist**
✅ Clean browser (no extensions visible)
✅ Full HD or 4K resolution
✅ Smooth cursor movements
✅ Pre-populate with realistic demo data
✅ Show loading states briefly
✅ Demonstrate error handling elegantly
✅ Feature smooth transitions between sections

### **Text Overlay Standards**
- **Headline Font:** Large, bold, high contrast
- **Subtitle Font:** Medium weight, clear
- **Animation:** Fade in or slide in (not jarring)
- **Duration:** Enough time to read comfortably (3-5 seconds minimum)
- **Position:** Lower third or centered (context-dependent)

## 💡 **TIPS FOR MAXIMUM IMPACT**

1. **Start with a hook** - Grab attention in first 3 seconds
2. **Show, don't tell** - Use actual platform footage
3. **Keep it moving** - No dead air or long pauses
4. **Professional quality** - Invest in good production
5. **Clear CTA** - Make next steps obvious
6. **Optimize for mobile** - Most views will be on phones
7. **Add captions** - Many watch without sound
8. **Test with audience** - Get feedback before final release
