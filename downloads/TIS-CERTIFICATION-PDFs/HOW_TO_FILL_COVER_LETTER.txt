UjenziXform — How to fill the KRA cover letter PDF
==================================================

The cover letter is a FILLABLE HTML form (not a static PDF template).

STEP 1 — Open in your browser (Edge or Chrome):
  D:\UjenziXform\downloads\TIS-ETIMS-Architecture\KRA_TIS_COVER_LETTER.html

STEP 2 — Click into each grey underline field and type:
  - Applicant KRA PIN
  - eTIMS portal reference number
  - Contact name, title, email, telephone
  - Registered address (pre-filled — confirm before printing)
  - Signatory name, title, signing date
  (Date at top-right is editable too)

STEP 3 — Sign in the box under "Yours faithfully" using finger (tablet/phone) or mouse.
  Use "Clear signature" to redraw if needed.

STEP 4 — Click the blue "Print / Save as PDF" button
  OR press Ctrl+P → Destination: Save as PDF → Save

STEP 5 — Save as:
  01_KRA_TIS_COVER_LETTER.pdf

STEP 6 — Copy that file to:
  D:\UjenziXform\downloads\TIS-OPTICAL-DISK\02-PDF-DOCUMENTS\

Website (pre-filled): https://ujenzi-pro.vercel.app/

Do NOT use the auto-generated empty PDF for KRA submission —
always fill the HTML form first, then save as PDF.
