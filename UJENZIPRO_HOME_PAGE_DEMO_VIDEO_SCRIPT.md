## 🎵 Audio & Music Guidelines

### **Background Music**:
- **Style**: Uplifting, modern, with subtle African influences
- **Tempo**: Moderate to upbeat (120-140 BPM)
- **Instruments**: Mix of modern electronic with traditional African percussion
- **Volume**: Background level, allowing narration to be clear

### **Sound Effects**:
- **Transitions**: Subtle whoosh sounds for scene changes
- **UI Interactions**: Soft click sounds for button highlights
- **Statistics**: Gentle chime sounds for counter animations
- **Success Stories**: Warm, positive notification sounds

### **Narration**:
- **Voice**: Professional, warm, Kenyan accent preferred
- **Pace**: Clear and measured, allowing for visual absorption
- **Tone**: Confident, inspiring, locally-relevant

## 📱 Technical Specifications

### **Video Format**:
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- **Frame Rate**: 30 FPS
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **File Size**: Optimized for web (under 50MB)

### **Screen Recording Settings**:
- **Browser**: Chrome or Safari for best rendering
- **Screen Resolution**: 1920x1080
- **Recording Software**: OBS Studio, Camtasia, or similar
- **Cursor Highlighting**: Enabled for user interaction points

### **Text Overlays**:
- **Font**: Modern, clean sans-serif (similar to site typography)
- **Colors**: Consistent with UjenziPro brand colors
- **Animation**: Subtle fade-in/fade-out effects
- **Duration**: 2-3 seconds per overlay
