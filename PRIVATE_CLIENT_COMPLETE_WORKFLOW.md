### **Phase 3: SHOPPING & PURCHASING** 🛒

#### **Method 1: Quick Direct Purchase (Individual Items)**

```
Suppliers Page → Suppliers Tab

1. Browse materials catalog
2. See materials with photos and prices
3. Click "Buy Now" (Green button) on any item
4. See: "✅ Adding to Cart"
5. Item added to cart
6. Continue shopping or checkout
```

#### **Method 2: Purchase Order (Multiple Items)**

```
Suppliers Page → Purchase Tab

1. See shopping cart wizard
   
   Step 1: SELECT MATERIALS
   ├─ Browse materials catalog
   ├─ Click "+ Add to Cart" on items
   ├─ Adjust quantities (+/-)
   ├─ See live cart total
   └─ Continue when ready
   
   Step 2: REVIEW CART
   ├─ See all selected items
   ├─ Verify quantities
   ├─ Check total amount
   ├─ Remove unwanted items
   └─ Continue to details
   
   Step 3: SUBMIT ORDER
   ├─ Enter project name
   ├─ Enter delivery address
   ├─ Select delivery date
   ├─ Add special notes
   ├─ Review order summary
   └─ Click "Submit Purchase Order"

2. ✅ Order submitted!
3. Wait for supplier quotes
4. Compare quotes
5. Accept best quote
6. Purchase complete!
```

## 💡 **Private Client Tips:**

### **Best Practices:**

**Shopping:**
- ✅ Use Purchase Order for full projects (saves time)
- ✅ Add all items at once (better quotes)
- ✅ Include delivery date (better planning)
- ✅ Add notes for special requirements

**Delivery:**
- ✅ Track with GPS (know when arriving)
- ✅ Scan QR codes (verify authenticity)
- ✅ Confirm quality (before accepting)
- ✅ Rate supplier (help others)

**Savings:**
- ✅ Compare multiple suppliers (better prices)
- ✅ Order in advance (avoid rush fees)
- ✅ Buy in bulk when possible (discounts)
- ✅ Check reviews (quality suppliers)
